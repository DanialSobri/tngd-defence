RISK SCORING POLICY (v1)

Purpose
- Score recipient trust from 0 to 100.
- Route payment action based on score bands.
- Preserve genuine SME transactions while reducing scam losses.

Recipient categories
1) Known bad
- Blacklisted by TNG, Bank Negara, NSRC, or CCID.
- Always hard block.

2) Known good
- Verified TNG merchant, SSM-registered business, government body, or utility.
- Usually allow, unless strong negative risk overrides.

3) Grey zone
- Unverified SME, individual seller, hawker, new account, or P2P recipient.
- Evaluate with confidence scoring (not binary only).

Signal scoring (additive)
- verified_tng_merchant: +40
- ssm_registered_business: +25
- account_age: +5 to +20
- transaction_history_volume: +5 to +20
- sender_prior_history_with_recipient: +30
- network_reputation: +10 to +20
- blacklist_match: -100
- mule_pattern_signals: -30 to -60
- recent_fraud_reports: -50
- suspicious_naming: -10 to -30

Scoring formula
- trust_score_raw = sum(all signal contributions)
- trust_score = clamp(trust_score_raw, 0, 100)

Routing decision
- 80 to 100: TRUSTED
  - action: ALLOW
  - user_message: Normal flow, no friction.

- 50 to 79: LIKELY_SAFE
  - action: ALLOW_WITH_WARNING
  - user_message: "First time paying this merchant. Confirm the amount."

- 30 to 49: UNCERTAIN
  - action: ALLOW_WITH_VERIFICATION
  - user_message: Additional verification required.

- 10 to 29: SUSPICIOUS
  - action: COOL_OFF_OR_GUARDIAN_VERIFICATION
  - user_message: Cooling-off and/or guardian verification is required.

- 0 to 9: HIGH_RISK
  - action: BLOCK
  - user_message: "This account has been flagged. Transfer cancelled."

Agent output contract (STRICT)
- Return JSON only.
- Do not wrap JSON in markdown fences.
- Do not include explanatory text outside JSON.
